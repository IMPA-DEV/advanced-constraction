FAQ

How many variation of kitchen styles are there and where can they be seen?

We have more than 73 variations of front facing panels and more than 200 variations of countertops. Most of them can be seen on our website. We can also help you build a kitchen that will match the general style of your house that would stand out against the kitchens of your friends and neighbors and collect their surprising reviews.

Why are our prices lower than the competition?

We do not have a middle man and we have our own production process. We do not work with outside subcontractors, and all of our employees are full time. All projects work is completed in our own shop.

What is the warranty for our work?

We guarantee our craftsmanship for 5 years which includes all materials and labor.

What determines the price of the project and what are some ways to save money?

The price of the project depends on type of materials and amount of labor is involved. Even at the design stage we alway recommend inexpensive, high quality and durable materials to reduce costs of repairs in the future. For example instead of marble or granite countertops, we can offer quartz or composite materials. It will be cheaper, but retain its strength and safety.
